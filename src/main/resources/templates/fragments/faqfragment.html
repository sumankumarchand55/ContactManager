How to find Register or login button ? ›

In the upper-right corner of the navigation bar, you'll see a rounded user icon. Click on it, and a dropdown menu will appear with options to log in or sign up.

How do I register or log in ? ›

Registering on Smart Contact Manager is easy and quick. You can either sign up manually by providing your details like name, email, and password, or you can opt for a faster option by signing up directly using your Google account. The Google sign-up allows you to bypass manual data entry and log in securely with a single click. Once you are registered, logging in is equally simple. Just use your credentials (email and password), or continue using your Google account for seamless and secure access. We’ve ensured that your login process remains secure with industry-standard authentication protocols.
